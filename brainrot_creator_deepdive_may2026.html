flags and considerations

  • Spotify conversion is broken. 22M+ monthly IG views producing only 17.6K monthly Spotify listeners is an anomaly. The "every video = my music" TikTok CTA is too soft — it names no track and gives no direct link. A pinned TikTok naming "brainrot baby" + Spotify link could move this number meaningfully within a single release cycle.
  • Release cadence has slipped. Her ~1/month pace implied a mid-May drop. "Be ur brainrot" presave exists but has no announced date. Momentum from a social spike without a new release to capture it is wasted.
  • "We are charlie kirk" (Feb 2026) is a politically coded track title in her catalog. Meaning — satire, homage, or otherwise — is ambiguous from public materials. Any mainstream brand must review the full catalog before signing. Streetwear, energy drink, and gaming brands will likely be unbothered; conservative FMCG brands should be flagged.
  • Unmanaged and publicly signalling it. Business contact is a personal Gmail. Spotify bio reads "labels are food and mommas hungry." No Night Media or any other management/label association found. The door is open — but the absence of a manager at this growth velocity is both an opportunity and a risk.
  • YouTube is a real asset. 66K subscribers and 2,200 videos was underdocumented in the prior profile. YouTube Membership ("Lid worden") is active, meaning she has a fourth direct-monetisation channel alongside Patreon, Twitch subs, and Throne. Shorts appear to be repurposed TikTok content, meaning minimal incremental production cost.
  • Subsidia: Dawn Vol. 11 ("Falling Hard," track 11/12, ℗ 2026 Subsidia) is her first label-adjacent placement. Subsidia is Excision's bass/dubstep imprint — a credible EDM industry foothold and a warm lead for further label or booking conversations.
  • Sponsor-virgin with growing leverage. No prior brand deals identified anywhere. First brand gets category exclusivity with zero audience fatigue. Rates set today will look cheap at 100K Instagram followers — that threshold is days away at current pace.
  • Audience age range. Self-described as "18yr old hyperpop leader" with confirmed Gen Z skew (16–24). Under-18s are likely present in the audience. Age-gated products (alcohol, gambling, vape, sportsbooks) are a regulatory and reputational risk regardless of her own age.
